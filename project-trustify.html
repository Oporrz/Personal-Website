Trustify - AI-Powered Product Authentication Platform

A production-grade cybersecurity platform designed to combat counterfeit products in food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic supply chains. Trustify leverages OCR, geospatial intelligence, and anomaly detection to generate real-time authenticity confidence scores (0–100%) while enabling public participation and government-level monitoring.

Case Structure

Problem

  • Consumer uncertainty in authenticity verification
  • Brand revenue loss from counterfeit zones
  • Reactive government enforcement
  • Lack of geospatial counterfeit clustering
  • Fraudulent reporting & spam abuse

Stakeholder Groups

  • Consumers: Mobile scanning & instant score
  • Brands: Batch monitoring & geographic risk analytics
  • Government: Red-zone heatmap & DBSCAN clustering

Results & Impact

For Consumers

  • Verification within 3–5 seconds

  • Reward-based engagement

For Brands

  • Early counterfeit detection

  • Revenue protection

For Government

  • Real-time risk zone identification

  • Trend analysis

Security Implementation

  • JWT authentication (7-day expiry)
  • Bcrypt password hashing
  • Helmet.js security headers
  • Rate limiting & login throttling
  • SQL injection & XSS protection
  • IP whitelisting & encrypted backups

System Functionality

1. OCR Product Verification

  • Image capture → OCR extraction → DB comparison
  • Weighted authenticity scoring model
  • Confidence classification (High / Medium / Risk)

2. Anti-Cheat System

  • Image hashing
  • Device fingerprinting
  • Rate limiting (30 scans/hour)
  • Login throttling

3. Gamification

  • Scan reward system
  • Counterfeit detection bonus
  • First-in-area bonus

4. Government Red Zone Monitoring

  • PostGIS DBSCAN clustering
  • 5km detection radius
