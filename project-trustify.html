Case Structure

Problem

Counterfeit products remain a major threat across Southeast Asia, impacting consumer safety, brand reputation, and national economies.

Key Challenges

1. Consumer Uncertainty

Consumers lack a fast and reliable way to verify product authenticity before purchase.

2. Brand Revenue Loss

Manufacturers struggle to detect counterfeit distribution zones early.

3. Reactive Government Enforcement

Authorities often rely on delayed reports rather than real-time intelligence.

4. Lack of Geospatial Insight

No centralized system clusters counterfeit reports to identify emerging red zones.

5. Fraudulent Reporting & Abuse

Existing systems are vulnerable to spam submissions and duplicate scans.

Our Case Study

To design Trustify, we structured the system around three stakeholder groups:

1. General Consumers

- Scan product labels using mobile camera

- Receive authenticity score within seconds

- Earn rewards for verified reports

2. Brands / Manufacturers

- Register product metadata

- Monitor suspicious batch/serial activity

- View geographic risk analytics

3. Government Agencies

- Real-time red zone heatmap

- Cluster detection using DBSCAN

- Trend monitoring dashboard
